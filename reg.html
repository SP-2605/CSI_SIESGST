Registration Procedure for Innovation

Registration Rules

  • Fill out the Google Form for the competition. Save your records for further use.
  • Visit abstract page to learn more about submitting your abstracts.
  • Registration fees is to be paid by the teams whose abstract is selected.
  • The registration cost for each team is ₹600 which covers 3 members, additional members will be charged ₹200 per head.
  • Only the team leader will be notified of their selection and further communication is to be carried out only with the team leaders.
  • Fill the google form before you submit the abstract else your submission will not be considered.
  • Abstract should be submitted from team leader's email address.
